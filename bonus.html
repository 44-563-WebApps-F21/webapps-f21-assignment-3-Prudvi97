The Green Guard

Green Guard most often wield bladed weaponry, including daggers, swords, axes, and spears. The Green Guard is also one of the few who receive special gear. The suit's outer layer was composed of a carborundum matrix alloy, and is equipped with hand-blasters that can fire high-frequency electrical green bolts, small missiles contained in the back shoulder area of the battle-suit.
